Event Packages & Bars

How it works

Events are never one size fits all so we offer pricing to fit your needs.

1.

First, let's craft your menu!

Let's get creative with your drink menu! Tell us your favorite spirits, mixers, and garnishes, and we'll help you design a signature cocktail menu that's as unique as your event. Don't forget to estimate your guest count and the duration of the event so we can ensure everyone has a glass in hand!

2.

Next, choose your mobile bar.

Browse our collection of stylish mobile bar carts and select the one that best fits the vibe of your event.

3.

Finally, customize your package.

Add a personal touch with customizations. From themed bar decor to branded cocktail napkins, let us know how we can tailor our service to make your event unforgettable. Once we have all the details, we’ll lock in your date and send you a confirmation. Cheers to an amazing event ahead!
