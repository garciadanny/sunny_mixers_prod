What To Expect

Our two-hour classes are designed to immerse you in the world of mixology.

  • Craft Three Signature Cocktails: Under our expert guidance, you'll learn how to mix, shake, and stir like a pro.
  • Educational & Fun Atmosphere: Discover the fascinating history behind each cocktail and its ingredients.
  • All-Inclusive Experience: We provide everything you need—recipe cards, a personal cocktail making kit, and top-quality ingredients.
  • Musical Vibes: Enjoy a curated playlist that keeps the energy high and the creativity flowing.

Whether you're here for a solo date night, fun outing with friends, a special date, or a corporate event, our classes cater to all.
